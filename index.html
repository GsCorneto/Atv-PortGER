Black Widow, the Redeemed Assassin

A human spy who was once part of Sauron's cult but escaped his grasp and now fights alongside the Free Peoples. She wields enchanted daggers and possesses stealth skills that rival the finest elves.

Iron Man, the Enchanted Blacksmith

A visionary dwarf from the Blue Mountains who crafted a magical suit of armor made of mithril and steel. He uses his intellect to create gadgets and weapons to fight against the forces of darkness.

Vision, the Guardian of the Soul Crystal

A mystical being created by the Valar to protect the Soul Crystal, an artifact that contains part of the essence of creation. He is made of solid light and can pass through walls and fly.

Hawkeye, the Guardian of the Forest

A human archer who guards the borders of Lothlórien and Mirkwood. His aim is legendary, and he uses enchanted arrows crafted by the elves.

Kingpin, the Merchant of Angmar

A massive and cunning human who became wealthy by controlling the illegal trade of dark artifacts. He finances the armies of Angmar in exchange for power and influence.

Ultron, the Mithril Automaton

A creation of the dwarves, forged from mithril and enchanted with a mind of its own. Ultron rebelled against his creators and seeks to exterminate all life to achieve "perfection."

Mephisto, the Temptor of Souls

A demonic spirit roaming Middle-earth, offering power in exchange for souls. He is a master of manipulation and has corrupted kings, elves, and even wizards.

Doctor Doom, the Tyrant of Latverion

A human necromancer, originally a king of a small realm in the eastern part of Middle-earth, who was corrupted by Morgoth. He wields enchanted armor and is a master of dark sorcery.
